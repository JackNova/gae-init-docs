Finalise Contact View

As a final touch in order to view the contact we'll modify the contact list so when you click on the name you should be redirected to that view.

Find the line that renders the name of the contact and replaced it with the following: 

...
<td>
  <a href="{{url_for('contact_view', contact_id=contact_db.key.id())}}">
    {{contact_db.name}}
  </a>
</td>
...

Now visit the contact list (http://localhost:8080/contact/), and you should be able to click on the name of each contact and view their details.
