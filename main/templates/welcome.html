gae-init is the easiest way to kick start new web applications on Google App Engine.

The docs are still in progress, so feel free to send feedback with your questions or just contribute.

Check it in action with live demos and get started:

gae-init gae-init-babel gae-init-upload gae-init-debug

Features

  • Open ID sign in
  • Secret keys are stored in the Datastore.
  • Automatic redirect back to where you were after sign in.
  • Force redirect by providing &next argument to the sign in URL.
  • JSON services with more URL, limits, filter & orders.
  • Feedback form
  • Much more...
